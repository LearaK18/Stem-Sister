🌟 Welcome! I’m Leara, and I built STEM Sister to open doors, spark curiosity, and empower every child — especially girls — to dream big and shine bright. 🌟

Opening Doors to Bright Futures

I created this platform to inspire and uplift underprivileged children, especially girls, by giving them free and exciting opportunities to explore STEM. Every child deserves a chance to shine, and I am here to make that possible.
