Meet Leara

Behind STEM Sister is a girl with a big dream: to make STEM fun, accessible, and inspiring for every child — especially girls who deserve to see themselves as future scientists, engineers, and creators.

My Story

Hello! I’m Leara Kapuriya, the creator of STEM Sister. Growing up, I often wished for a space where learning science and technology felt exciting, colorful, and welcoming. That’s why I built this platform — to share knowledge, spark curiosity, and encourage children to believe in their potential.

Through STEM Sister, I want to break barriers and show that learning can be joyful. Whether it’s solving math puzzles, experimenting with science, coding something new, or exploring creativity through art, I believe every child deserves the chance to shine.

My Mission

Inspire Curiosity

Encouraging children to ask questions, explore ideas, and discover the joy of learning.

Empower Girls

Creating a safe, supportive space where girls feel confident to pursue STEM dreams.

Build Access

Making resources available to underprivileged children so no one is left behind.

Fun Facts About Me

🎨 My favorite color palette is purple + pink + teal.

🌍 My dream is to see more girls leading in STEM fields worldwide.
